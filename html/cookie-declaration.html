What Are Cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that a website saves on your computer, mobile, or other device when you visit the site. Cookies provide a way for a website to recognize you and keep track of your actions and preferences. Applicable laws state that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for our website to operate. For all other types of cookies we will ask for your permission.
