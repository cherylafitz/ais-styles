Cookie Declaration

We care about the protection and privacy of your and our applicants' data. To that end, we limit the number of small data files called cookies that we place on your device. We only use cookies that are necessary to make our website work properly and to analyze our traffic. They are removed after you finish your AIS session. We do not store any personally-identifiable information in cookies.

This declaration explains how we use cookies and the choices you have. Except as otherwise stated in this declaration, our Privacy Policy will apply to our processing of the data we collect via cookies.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of text that a website saves on your computer, mobile, or other device when you visit the site. Cookies are used to store and receive identifiers and other information. Cookies provide a way for a website to recognize you and keep track of your actions and preferences. Applicable laws state that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for our website to operate. For all other types of cookies we will ask for your permission.

Which Cookies Do We Use and Why?

Cookies help us provide and improve AIS, such as by personalizing content. While the cookies that we use may change from time to time as we improve and update AIS, we use them for the following purposes.

We use cookies to verify your account and determine when you are logged in. That way, we can make it easier for you to access AIS and show you the appropriate experience and features. For example, we use cookies to keep you logged in as you navigate between AIS pages.

Direct, necessary, session cookies used by AIS
